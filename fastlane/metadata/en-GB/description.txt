LIVE COUNTER
The moment you open Earnza, your income starts accumulating on screen. Per-minute, per-hour, per-session — it ticks up in real time. A number that once felt distant becomes visceral and personal. Share milestone moments when you hit meaningful thresholds.

OBJECTS — PRICE THINGS IN WORK HOURS
Browse 30+ everyday items — from coffee to flights to rent — priced not in money but in work time. See how many you earn per hour. See how long you'd need to work to afford a new phone. Create unlimited custom objects. Add anything that matters to you.

CITIES — WHERE DOES YOUR SALARY STRETCH?
The same salary means something very different in Austin versus San Francisco, London versus Lisbon. Compare your income across 50+ cities worldwide. Sort by housing pressure, tech affordability, daily purchasing power, or lifestyle fit. Pin favourites for quick reference.

WORK CANVAS — FEEL THE LABOUR COST
See purchases as stacked work-hour blocks. Abstract prices become concrete effort. That weekend trip isn't a pound amount — it's a precise count of hours from your life. This single view changes how you think about spending.

GAP — COMPARE THE PACE
Add a comparator salary — a competing offer, your manager's package, a peer's wage. Watch both accumulate in real time. See the exact delta per hour and how long it takes them to earn what you earn in a full day.

SCENARIOS — PLAN CAREER DECISIONS
Save multiple salary setups. Evaluate a job offer in a new city. Compare gross versus take-home. Duplicate scenarios, tweak assumptions, compare side by side. Earnza is a thinking tool built for every salary decision you'll ever make.

SHARE STUDIO
