You've been alive for over a billion heartbeats. Do you know exactly how many?

Lunivo turns your birthday into a living dashboard of the numbers your life produces — continuously updated, mathematically precise, and unlike anything you've seen in an app before.

WHAT IS LUNIVO?

Enter your birthday. That's it. Lunivo instantly calculates 30+ statistics across five categories — Body, Time, Space, Life, and Absurd — and keeps them running in real time. Everything is backed by physics, astronomical constants, or carefully sourced lifestyle estimates.

YOUR BODY

Your heart has beaten over a billion times. You've taken hundreds of millions of breaths. You've slept through roughly a third of your life, dreamed thousands of dreams, blinked tens of millions of times. Lunivo shows you the full count — ticking upward, right now.

TIME & SPACE

You've completed multiple orbits around the Sun. Hundreds of full moons have risen since you were born. You've traveled billions of kilometers through space just by standing on this planet. Lunivo calculates it all using the same constants astronomers use — to the kilometer, to the second.

LIFE

Meals eaten. Words spoken. Steps walked. Songs heard. Glasses of water. Phone unlocks. Coffees. Laughs. Lunivo estimates the everyday scale of a human life using well-researched averages. Every estimate is clearly labeled — no false precision.

ABSURD

Mondays Survived. Books' Worth of Words Spoken. Breaths Taken Despite Everything. The Absurd category reframes your real stats with a little existential humor. The math is the same. The perspective is different.

MILESTONES

Every life contains approaching round-number milestones: your 1,000th full moon, your 1 billionth heartbeat, your 10,000th day. Lunivo tracks each one — showing the estimated arrival date, how fast you're approaching it, and your current progress.

SHAREABLE CARDS

Export any stat as a beautifully designed card and share it anywhere. Choose your template, pick your format — story or square — and send directly to your camera roll, Instagram, Messages, or anywhere else. The numbers are real, and they're uniquely yours.

DESIGNED WITH CARE

Four hand-crafted visual themes: Light, Dark, Solar, and Mono. A cosmic animated background. Glass-morphism stat cards with rolling number animations that update live. Configurable display density, motion preferences, background intensity, and text size.

Lunivo is built to feel as precise as the numbers it shows.

COMPLETELY PRIVATE

No account. No server. No internet connection required — ever. Your birthday never leaves your device. All 30+ statistics are calculated locally, offline, always.

HOW IT WORKS

Tap any stat card to open its full detail view. See the exact formula used to calculate the number, the source of each constant, and whether the value is exact or estimated. The complete Methodology reference is always one tap away.

Exact stats — like seconds lived, leap days, or Earth orbits — use precise calendar arithmetic and verified astronomical constants. Lifestyle estimates — like meals or steps — use well-researched global averages and are always marked clearly.

YOUR LIFE, AT SCALE

Most apps focus on what you haven't done yet. Lunivo shows you what you've already accomplished — in heartbeats, in orbits, in breaths, in kilometers traveled through space — and leaves the meaning entirely to you.
